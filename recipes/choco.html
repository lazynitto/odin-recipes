Chocolate Cake Recipe

A freshly baked chocolate cake

To make a simple, rich chocolate cake, begin by whisking together the dry ingredients: two cups of sugar, one and three-quarters cups of flour, three-quarters cup of cocoa powder, and a teaspoon and a half each of baking powder and baking soda.

Next, beat in two eggs, one cup of milk, a half-cup of vegetable oil, and two teaspoons of vanilla extract until the batter is smooth. Slowly stir in one cup of boiling water or hot coffee—this thins the batter but blooms the cocoa powder for an intensely deep chocolate flavor.

Pour the mixture into two greased 9-inch pans and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30 to 35 minutes until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, then cool before frosting.

Ingredients

The Dry Ingredients

  • 2 cups Granulated sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups All-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking soda
  • 1 tsp Salt

The Wet Ingredients

  • 2 large Eggs
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1/2 cup Vegetable oil (or canola oil)
  • 2 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 cup Boiling water or hot brewed coffee (coffee intensifies the chocolate flavor without tasting like coffee!)

Step-by-step guide for baking the chocolate cake

  1. Prep Oven and Pans: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease two 9-inch cake pans.
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: Whisk together the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Add Wet Ingredients: Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat for 2 minutes until smooth.
  4. Stir in Hot Water: Slowly stir in the boiling water or hot coffee. (The batter will be thin!).
  5. Bake and Cool: Pour into pans and bake for 30 to 35 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean. Let cool completely.
